Atlas of Mortality due to Rare Diseases

This atlas shows the population-based mortality attributed to Rare Diseases, its time trends and geographic variability.

The epidemiological information is presented as a complement to the data from existing Rare Diseases registries.

Data sources

Spanish population and mortality data were obtained from National Statistics Institute (INE), according to cause of death, residence place, sex and age.

The causes of death attributed to Rare Diseases were selected according to ICD 10 list of the Spanish Rare Diseases Registries Research Network (SpainRDR).

Basemaps were obtained from Instituto Geográfico Nacional, Centro Nacional de Información Geográfica or municipalities, provinces and Autonomous Communities and from Ministerio de Agricultura, Pesca y Alimentación for districts.
